Curriculum teaches on advanced topics within CS, from operating systems to Data Structure, although some crucial subjects are rarely covered, hence students are left to cover them on their own. Usage of tools such as terminal based editors, collaboration tools like github/gitlab are not addressed in class althogh they are also so crucial things to learn so as to adapt the standards of the industry.

In response to the situation students have to spend a lot of hours to learn and gain experience on the tools and technologies that are important for their success in the industry of which it mostly has to cost them time after they have completed their time in school.
