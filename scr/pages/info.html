Reason for the Site

Traditionally, its true that most of the computer science student expect that what is delivered in the class is the key to delivering products or solution once they are out of the college.
something that is not really in the current world.

From the survey we had for a year, came to notice there is a large number of things that are important for CS students as they are preparing for to tackle the Industry after they are done with School.

Withiout blaming the curriculum as its impossible for it to have everything covered, we decided to start this program of putting together some good resources that could be helpfull to students in the CS particularly things that are missing in the Curriculum.
